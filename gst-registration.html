What is GST Registration?

GST or Goods and Services Tax is a unified indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services across India. GST registration is the process by which a business or individual obtains a unique 15-digit GSTIN — Goods and Services Tax Identification Number — from the Government of India. This GSTIN is mandatory for legally collecting GST from customers, issuing GST invoices and filing GST returns on the official GST portal.

GST registration replaced multiple indirect taxes like VAT, service tax, excise duty and octroi with a single unified tax system. Any business in Howrah or anywhere in West Bengal that crosses the prescribed turnover threshold — or falls under a mandatory category — must complete GST registration before commencing taxable supply of goods or services.

💡 Important: Businesses with annual turnover above ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states) are mandatorily required to register for GST. For e-commerce sellers and inter-state suppliers the threshold does not apply — registration is mandatory regardless of turnover.

Who Needs GST Registration?

  • Businesses and traders with annual turnover above ₹20 lakhs in goods or services — ₹10 lakhs for special category states
  • E-commerce sellers selling on Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Snapdeal or any other online marketplace — mandatory regardless of turnover
  • Inter-state suppliers of goods and services supplying outside West Bengal to any other state in India
  • Casual taxable persons conducting temporary business activities or seasonal trade in India
  • Non-resident taxable persons supplying goods or services in India without a fixed place of business
  • Agents and brokers supplying goods or services on behalf of a registered principal
  • Input service distributors distributing input tax credit across multiple business branches
  • Persons liable to pay tax under reverse charge mechanism — RCM
  • Businesses registered under any earlier indirect tax law such as VAT, service tax or excise duty
  • Any person or business voluntarily wanting to avail Input Tax Credit benefits and issue GST invoices

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the applicant — individual, proprietor, partner, director or authorised signatory
  • Aadhaar Card of the applicant — mandatory for Aadhaar authentication during GST registration
  • Passport size photograph of the proprietor, partner or director
  • Proof of business address — latest electricity bill, municipal tax receipt, rent agreement or property ownership document
  • Bank account details — cancelled cheque, first page of passbook or bank statement showing account number and IFSC code
  • Business registration certificate if available — Shop and Establishment Act License, MSME Udyam certificate or trade License
  • Partnership deed — for partnership firms registering for GST
  • Certificate of Incorporation and MOA or AOA — for private limited companies and LLPs
  • Board resolution or authorisation letter — for companies authorising a signatory for GST registration
  • Digital Signature Certificate — DSC — mandatory for companies and LLPs filing GST registration online

Our Process

  1. Document Collection: Share your documents with us in person at our Howrah or Kolkata office or send scanned copies digitally. We provide a precise checklist based on your business type so you never have to guess what is needed.
  2. Document Verification: Our GST experts verify every document for accuracy, completeness and compliance before proceeding. This step ensures your application is rejected free and processed smoothly without delays.
  3. Application Preparation: We accurately fill in your GST registration application — Form GST REG-01 — on the official GST portal with all required business details, HSN codes, business nature and authorised signatory information.
  4. Aadhaar Authentication: We guide you through the mandatory Aadhaar OTP based authentication process on the GST portal — a required step introduced by the Government of India for all new GST registrations.
  5. Application Filing and ARN Generation: The completed application is submitted on the GST portal. An ARN — Application Reference Number — is generated immediately upon successful submission which can be used to track your application status.
  6. GST Officer Verification: The GST officer reviews your application and documents. If any clarification or additional document is required we handle the response promptly on your behalf to avoid any delay in processing.
  7. GSTIN and Certificate Delivery: Upon successful verification your unique 15-digit GSTIN and GST registration certificate in Form GST REG-06 is issued by the GST portal — typically within 3 to 7 working days of application submission.

Benefits of GST Registration

  • Legal authorisation to collect GST from customers and issue valid GST tax invoices for all taxable supplies of goods and services
  • Avail Input Tax Credit — ITC — on GST paid on business purchases, raw materials, capital goods and services thereby reducing overall tax liability significantly
  • Expand business freely across state borders — inter-state supply of goods and services becomes legally permissible only after GST registration
  • Sell on major e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho and Snapdeal — all these platforms mandatorily require a valid GSTIN before onboarding any seller
  • Enhanced business credibility and trust among buyers, vendors, banks and financial institutions — a GSTIN signals a legitimate and compliant business
  • Access to government tenders, contracts and procurement opportunities — most government bodies require GST registration as a mandatory eligibility criterion
  • Easier access to business loans and working capital finance — banks and NBFCs consider GST registration and GST return filing history as key eligibility criteria
  • Participate in the formal economy and build a strong financial track record through regular GST return filing — essential for future business growth and expansion
  • Avoid heavy penalties, interest and legal action — unregistered businesses found liable for GST registration face penalties of up to 100% of tax due under the CGST Act 2017
  • Voluntary GST registration even below the threshold limit allows small businesses to avail ITC benefits and project a more professional and credible business image

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnover limit for mandatory GST registration in West Bengal?

In West Bengal businesses supplying goods with annual turnover above ₹40 lakhs and businesses supplying services with annual turnover above ₹20 lakhs are mandatorily required to obtain GST registration. However for e-commerce sellers, inter-state suppliers, casual taxable persons and certain other categories GST registration is mandatory regardless of turnover.

How long does GST registration take in Howrah?

GST registration typically takes 3 to 7 working days after all documents are submitted correctly on the GST portal. If Aadhaar authentication is successfully completed the registration may be granted within 3 working days. In case the GST officer raises a query or requests additional documents the process may take up to 30 days. Our team ensures all documents are correct and complete before filing to avoid any unnecessary delays.

Is GST registration free or does it have a government fee?

GST registration itself is completely free of cost — there is no government fee for obtaining a GSTIN. The GST portal allows free online registration for all eligible businesses and individuals. Our professional service charges for end-to-end GST registration assistance are separate and completely transparent with no hidden costs.

Can I operate multiple businesses under one GST registration?

A single GST registration covers all business activities carried out from the same state. If you have multiple business verticals operating from West Bengal you can register them under one GSTIN. However if you have business operations in multiple states a separate GST registration is mandatory for each state where you have a place of business or supply goods and services.

What happens if I do not register for GST despite being liable?

Operating a GST liable business without registration is a serious offence under the CGST Act 2017. Penalties include 100% of the tax amount due as penalty in cases of deliberate tax evasion and 10% of tax due subject to a minimum of ₹10,000 in other cases. Additionally the GST officer has powers to conduct search, seizure and arrest in cases of serious non compliance. It is always safer and more cost effective to register on time.

Can I cancel my GST registration if my turnover falls below the threshold?

Yes. If your annual turnover falls below the mandatory GST registration threshold you can apply for voluntary cancellation of your GST registration on the GST portal using Form GST REG-16. The GST officer will process the cancellation after verifying that all pending GST returns have been filed and all outstanding tax dues have been cleared. Our team can assist you with the complete GST cancellation process.
