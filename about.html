Who We Are

About Bharat Tax Consultants Ranihati Howrah West Bengal

Bharat Tax Consultants is a trusted tax and financial advisory firm serving individuals and businesses across Ranihati, Howrah and West Bengal — with over 45 years of proven expertise in GST, income tax, compliance and smart investment solutions.

Building Trust, One Client at a Time

Bharat Tax Consultants was established with a singular vision — to make professional tax and financial advisory services accessible, affordable, and trustworthy for every individual, business, and entrepreneur in Kolkata and the surrounding region. Founded by Shyamal Sen, the firm was built on a foundation of accuracy, compliance, and client confidence at a time when navigating India's tax landscape demanded both expertise and personal attention.

Over the years, Bharat Tax Consultants has grown into a full-service consultancy trusted by thousands of clients — from salaried professionals and self-employed individuals to MSMEs, startups, and partnership firms. With the opening of our Howrah branch under the guidance of Manish Sau, we expanded our reach across West Bengal, adding services in GST registration, TDS return filing, company incorporation, trademark registration, FSSAI licensing, and investment advisory including Mutual Funds, IPO, NFO, and NCD.

Our mission is simple: to be the most reliable, transparent, and client-friendly tax consultancy in West Bengal — ensuring every client remains fully compliant, financially informed, and stress-free throughout the year.
