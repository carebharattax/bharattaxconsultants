Professional Tax & Trade License Services

Bharat Tax Consultants provides end-to-end assistance for Professional Tax (P. Tax) registration, enrolment, return filing, and Trade License application in Howrah and West Bengal. Both registrations are statutory obligations for businesses operating in West Bengal and non-compliance attracts penalties.

💡 Important: Professional Tax is a state-level tax governed by the West Bengal State Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1979. Every employer and self-employed individual earning above the prescribed threshold must register and pay P. Tax.

1. Professional Tax (P. Tax)

Professional Tax is levied by the Government of West Bengal on individuals earning income through employment, profession, trade, or calling. There are two types of registrations — Employer Registration (for businesses deducting P. Tax from employees' salaries) and Enrolment Certificate (for self-employed professionals and business owners).

Who Must Register for Professional Tax?

  • All employers — companies, LLPs, partnership firms, proprietorships — who have salaried employees in West Bengal
  • Self-employed professionals such as doctors, lawyers, chartered accountants, consultants, and engineers
  • Traders, shopkeepers, and business owners conducting trade or commerce in West Bengal
  • Directors of companies who draw remuneration or salary
  • Individuals earning above ₹10,000 per month from any profession or trade in West Bengal

P. Tax Rate Slab (West Bengal)

  • Up to ₹10,000 per month — Nil
  • ₹10,001 to ₹15,000 per month — ₹110 per month
  • ₹15,001 to ₹25,000 per month — ₹130 per month
  • ₹25,001 to ₹40,000 per month — ₹150 per month
  • Above ₹40,000 per month — ₹200 per month (maximum ₹2,500 per year)

Documents Required for P. Tax Registration

  • PAN Card of the proprietor / partners / directors
  • Aadhaar Card of the applicant
  • Photograph of the applicant
  • Business registration proof (GST certificate, trade license, or shop act)
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque
  • List of employees with salary details (for employer registration)
  • Address proof of business premises

P. Tax Return Filing

Registered employers must file monthly or annual P. Tax returns and deposit the tax deducted from employees to the West Bengal government. Self-employed individuals file and pay P. Tax annually. Late filing attracts interest and penalties. Our team handles return preparation and timely submission on your behalf.

💡 Note: Professional Tax paid is fully deductible as a business expense under the Income Tax Act, reducing your taxable income. Employees can also claim deduction under Section 16 of the Income Tax Act.

2. Trade License

A Trade License is a certificate issued by the local municipal authority (such as Howrah Municipal Corporation) that grants permission to carry out a specific trade or business from a particular premises. It is mandatory for all businesses operating within the municipal limits of West Bengal.

Who Needs a Trade License?

  • Shops, showrooms, retail outlets, and commercial establishments
  • Restaurants, hotels, food stalls, and eateries
  • Manufacturing units, workshops, and small industries
  • Medical clinics, diagnostic centres, and pharmacies
  • Service providers — salons, garages, coaching centres, and agencies
  • Any business operating from a fixed commercial or residential premises

Documents Required for Trade License

  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of the proprietor / authorised signatory
  • Passport-size photograph of the applicant
  • Proof of business address — rent agreement or ownership document
  • NOC from the landlord (if premises is rented)
  • Building plan / floor plan of the premises (if required by the municipal body)
  • Nature of business / trade description
  • GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)

Trade License Renewal

A Trade License is typically valid for one financial year (April to March) and must be renewed annually before 31st March. Failure to renew on time attracts a late fee and may lead to cancellation of the license. We assist in both fresh applications and timely annual renewals.

💡 Remember: Operating a business without a valid Trade License is a punishable offence under municipal laws and can lead to closure notices. Many banks and government departments also require a Trade License as a KYC document for business accounts and loan applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Professional Tax mandatory in West Bengal?

Yes. Professional Tax is mandatory for all employers and self-employed individuals earning above the prescribed threshold in West Bengal. Non-registration and non-payment attract penalties under the West Bengal State Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1979.

What is the difference between Employer Registration and Enrolment Certificate in P. Tax?

An Employer Registration (also called Registration Certificate or RC) is obtained by businesses that employ staff and are required to deduct P. Tax from employee salaries and deposit it with the government. An Enrolment Certificate (EC) is for self-employed individuals and business owners who pay P. Tax on their own income. Both are issued by the West Bengal Directorate of Commercial Taxes.

How long does it take to get a Trade License in Howrah?

A Trade License from Howrah Municipal Corporation is typically issued within 7 to 15 working days after submission of the complete application and payment of the prescribed fee. The timeline may vary depending on the nature of the business and any site inspection requirements by the municipal authority.

Can I apply for a Trade License for a home-based business?

Yes, in many cases a Trade License can be obtained for a home-based business, subject to the municipal authority's guidelines and the nature of the trade. An NOC from the landlord or co-owners may be required. Our team will guide you on the specific requirements applicable to your business type and location.

What happens if I miss the Trade License renewal deadline?

Failure to renew the Trade License before 31st March results in a late renewal penalty imposed by the municipal authority. Continued operation without a valid license can lead to notices, fines, or business closure orders. We send timely reminders and handle renewals on behalf of our clients to avoid such situations.
